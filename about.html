PAN Learning Center

History

PAN Learning Centre (PLC), is a full subsidiary of PAN (Peugeot) Nigeria Limited, the oldest car manufacturing and assembly plant in Nigeria.
PAN Learning Centre was established in 1978 with 11 expatriates under Organization for Rehabilitation through Training-ORT based in London. The World ORT Union based in Geneva Switzerland signed recruitment and training agreement with PAN in 1978 to recruit and train Nigerian senior staff and high level technical manpower for eventual indigenization of company’s operations.The Centre has since October 1989 been fully Nationalised. PLC is now open to the Nigerian Automotive Industry and the general public for trainings in Automotive Technology and other Auto related trades.
