The programme is intended to produce Innovative Technicians in Welding and Fabrication for Innovation Enterprises, Self-Employment, Private and Public Sectors of Nigerian Economy

Entry Requirements:

Candidate should score 100 and above in the UTME and possess a minimum of five (5) o'Level credits including Mathematics, English Language, Physics and Chemistry.

Duration:

The programme runs for a minimum of two academic sessions, delivered in span of two (2) years academic. This compromises of four (4) academic semesters and mandatory supervised industrial work experience (SIWES).
